About Us



Supra Web Design was founded in 2023 after we noticed the inflated costs faced by business owners looking to give their business a website, with many web design companies adding unnecessary features at added price. We aim to address the need for affordable, practical websites for businesses, especially small businesses. Headquartered in Darwin, Australia, we pride ourselves on the quality of our products and our customer-first, transparent approach to service.
